Privacy Policy

About This Policy
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data.
Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile information
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, pages visited, and features used
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser information
  • Location Data: General location information (with your consent)
  • Preferences: Your saved universities, wishlists, and search history
How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our university search services
  • Personalize your experience and show relevant recommendations
  • Improve our app functionality and user interface
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Usage data is typically retained for 2 years
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • Data may be retained longer if required by law
Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures
  • Employee training on data protection practices
Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and payment processors
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Business Partners: Only with your explicit consent
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymous statistics for research purposes
Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing
Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will notify you through the app or email
  • Major changes will require your consent
  • The updated policy will be posted on our website
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
